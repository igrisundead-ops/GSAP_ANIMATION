# Subject Ring Authority Beat

## Primary Use Case
Use this beat when introducing a human subject, founder, operator, expert, customer, or hero figure with authority framing.

## Best Used For
- founder intros
- expert commentary
- stress / struggle setup
- business authority compositions
- portrait-led explanation moments

## Visual Behavior
An oversized circular framing element enters first, establishing structure and authority. The subject arrives from below-left with blur resolve and a soft settle. The headline confirms the context after the frame is visually anchored.

## Motion Sequence
1. oversized ring enters and frames the composition
2. subject rises in with blur resolve
3. copy enters after the subject is visually established

## Emotional Effect
The beat feels serious, premium, controlled, and intentional. It adds status and compositional weight.

## Works Best With
- portraits
- founder cutouts
- operator imagery
- expert silhouettes
- case-study scenes
- problem escalation language

## Avoid Using When
Avoid for playful, youthful, or highly energetic sequences. This is an authority beat, not a hype beat.

## Customizable Elements
- subject slot
- ring size
- headline text
- ring opacity
- arrival direction
- timing
- blur intensity

## Retrieval Keywords
portrait authority beat, oversized ring framing, subject intro cinematic, business operator reveal, expert visual opener

## Agent Notes
This beat is good before diagrams, metrics, targets, or supporting icons. It works especially well as the first half of a two-part escalation scene.
