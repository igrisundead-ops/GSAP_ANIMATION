# Identity Profile

## Primary Use Case

A mature editorial beat for communicating memorability, identity, recognition, and distinctive positioning.

## Best Used For

- brand identity narratives
- founder-led edits
- recognition-based messaging
- premium editorial storytelling
- positioning campaigns

## Visual Behavior

A large side-profile asset occupies the right side of the composition while text anchors the lower left. This creates a stronger fashion-editorial layout than a centered hero approach.

## Motion Sequence

1. Side-profile asset slides in from the right with blur resolve
2. Asset settles into an asymmetrical editorial layout
3. Kicker enters
4. Headline staggers in
5. Soft idle drift keeps the frame alive

## Emotional Effect

- sophistication
- memorability
- calm authority
- editorial polish
- intentionality

## Works Best With

- sculptural heads
- founder profiles
- stylized portraits
- luxury visuals
- profile-oriented cutouts

## Avoid Using When

- the scene needs centered symmetry
- the story demands literal explanation
- the tone should feel playful
