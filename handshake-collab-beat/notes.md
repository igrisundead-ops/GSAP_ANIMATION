# Handshake Collab Beat

## Primary Use Case
Use this beat to transition from tension or confusion into partnership, offer, invitation, collaboration, or solution framing.

## Best Used For
- collaboration calls
- agency offer scenes
- partnership language
- sales CTA mid-sequences
- trust-building shifts after a problem beat

## Visual Behavior
A wider central asset enters from the right with blur resolve and soft settle. Supporting organic detail remains subtle. The offer text resolves after the asset arrives.

## Motion Sequence
1. central trust-oriented asset enters
2. small support accent resolves
3. offer/collaboration text appears

## Emotional Effect
This beat introduces trust, openness, possibility, and human agreement. It softens the sequence after pressure or strategic failure beats.

## Works Best With
- handshake visuals
- call booking imagery
- conversation/product demos
- collaboration language
- contact or CTA messaging

## Avoid Using When
Avoid as the first scene in a sequence. It works better after a tension beat or before a brand close.

## Customizable Elements
- main asset slot
- headline text
- support accent
- entry side
- hold duration
- scale settle

## Retrieval Keywords
collaboration beat, trust transition, handshake reveal, offer scene, partnership motion beat

## Agent Notes
This beat is usually the emotional pivot from problem to solution. Keep it restrained and warm.
