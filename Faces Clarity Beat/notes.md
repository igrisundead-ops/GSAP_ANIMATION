# Faces Clarity

## Primary Use Case

A clarification beat that simplifies the message after a stronger contrast moment. It communicates familiarity, humanity, and recognition.

## Best Used For

- trust-building narratives
- personal branding edits
- human-centered service messaging
- “buy from people” style storytelling
- social brand philosophy content

## Visual Behavior

A single face-oriented asset enters in a cleaner, more distilled composition. The frame is calmer than the rejection beat and focuses attention on one simple idea.

## Motion Sequence

1. Face asset resolves in with blur and slight rotation
2. Asset settles into a premium center composition
3. Kicker enters
4. Headline animates with restrained stagger

## Emotional Effect

- clarity
- familiarity
- softness
- reassurance
- human connection

## Works Best With

- sculptural faces
- portrait cutouts
- brand faces
- beauty imagery
- simple human metaphors

## Avoid Using When

- the message needs aggressive pacing
- the frame needs multiple competing elements
- the tone should feel loud or highly kinetic

## Customizable Elements

- face asset
- angle of entry
- headline
- kicker
- asset scale
- color grade
- blur amount

## Agent Notes

This beat works best after a contrast beat. It acts as a calming, clarifying response.
