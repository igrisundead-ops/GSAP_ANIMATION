# Vision Eye

## Primary Use Case

A symbolic focus beat for communicating vision, targeting, precision, creative direction, or future-oriented thinking.

## Best Used For

- strategic vision messaging
- brand positioning narratives
- creative direction storytelling
- precision / targeting metaphors
- foresight and clarity content

## Visual Behavior

An eye or lens asset enters centrally with blur resolve. Supporting guide lines extend outward and a viewfinder frame appears around the center, reinforcing focus and intent.

## Motion Sequence

1. Central eye asset enters upward with blur resolve
2. Guide lines animate outward
3. Viewfinder frame resolves into place
4. Kicker appears
5. Headline staggers in

## Emotional Effect

- focus
- precision
- intent
- clarity
- control

## Works Best With

- eye imagery
- camera lens imagery
- target icons
- precision metaphors
- minimal centered compositions

## Avoid Using When

- the story is casual or purely emotional
- the frame already contains many supporting objects
- the brand style is noisy or chaotic

## Customizable Elements

- eye asset
- line count
- line angles
- viewfinder size
- headline
- kicker
- blur amount
- timing
