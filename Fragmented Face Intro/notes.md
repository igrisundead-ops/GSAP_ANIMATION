# Fragmented Face Intro

## Primary Use Case

A symbolic opening beat for premium vertical storytelling. Best used when the message is about perception, identity, recognition, distortion, or first impression.

## Best Used For

- brand philosophy intros
- recognition / visibility messaging
- identity-driven short-form ads
- Pinterest save-style visuals
- cinematic editorial vertical sequences

## Visual Behavior

A central face or sculptural asset enters from below with blur resolve and controlled scale-up. Fragment particles peel from one side, implying fragmentation, distortion, or incomplete perception. Copy lands after the asset establishes visual dominance.

## Motion Sequence

1. Main face asset enters with blur resolve
2. Position settles into center composition
3. Fragment particles break outward from one side
4. Kicker fades upward into place
5. Headline animates with premium stagger
6. Subtle idle float keeps the frame alive

## Emotional Effect

- intrigue
- mystery
- recognition
- tension
- sophistication

## Works Best With

- faces
- masks
- sculptural portraits
- beauty objects
- identity metaphors
- minimal editorial backgrounds

## Avoid Using When

- the message is energetic and playful
- the scene needs loud motion graphics
- the brand tone is comedic or chaotic

## Customizable Elements

- main image
- particle density
- headline
- kicker
- accent color
- blur strength
- entrance duration
- idle float intensity
