# Strategy Chess

## Primary Use Case

A symbolic center-weighted beat for communicating planning, deliberateness, positioning, and competitive thinking.

## Best Used For

- strategy messaging
- business storytelling
- founder thought pieces
- agency capability narratives
- tactical brand sequences

## Visual Behavior

A single chess asset enters into a centered composition with blur resolve and clean scale-up. The frame becomes more minimal and cerebral than the surrounding beats.

## Motion Sequence

1. Chess asset rises into center frame
2. Blur resolves into a clean silhouette
3. Kicker enters
4. Headline reveals with premium stagger

## Emotional Effect

- intelligence
- control
- foresight
- seriousness
- discipline

## Works Best With

- chess pieces
- abstract tactical symbols
- strategic metaphors
- centered minimal layouts

## Avoid Using When

- the narrative is emotional rather than analytical
- the brand tone is casual and playful
- the scene needs multiple assets

## Customizable Elements

- central symbol
- headline
- kicker
- scale
- blur
- timing
- vertical position

strategy chess beat, tactical symbol reveal, cerebral motion beat, centered metaphor animation, deliberate brand storytelling
