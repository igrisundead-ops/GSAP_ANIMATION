# Content Cards

## Primary Use Case

A proof-of-output beat that shifts the story from metaphor into visible deliverables, examples, or portfolio-like results.

## Best Used For

- content agency promos
- creator service edits
- social proof visuals
- portfolio sequences
- output demonstration beats

## Visual Behavior

Three rounded cards rise upward into a centered composition with soft blur resolve. The motion is clean and premium rather than flashy. The frame feels like a visual proof moment.

## Motion Sequence

1. Card stack enters upward with stagger
2. Cards settle into clean side-by-side layout
3. Kicker appears
4. Headline animates with staggered type reveal

## Emotional Effect

- evidence
- credibility
- execution
- polish
- deliverability

## Works Best With

- portfolio screenshots
- thumbnails
- social post previews
- case study visuals
- product examples

## Avoid Using When

- the story should stay purely symbolic
- you do not have strong card assets
- the narrative is emotional rather than proof-driven

## Customizable Elements

- number of cards
- card dimensions
- corner radius
- card imagery
- stagger timing
- headline
- kicker
- shadow intensity

## Retrieval Keywords

content card reveal, proof-of-output beat, portfolio cards animation, social proof cards vertical, deliverable showcase motion

This is a practical proof beat and should be reusable across agency, creator, SaaS, and portfolio narratives.
